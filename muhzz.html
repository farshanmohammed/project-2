SIR VIV RICHARDS

Sir Isaac Vivian Alexander Richards KNH OBE OOC (born 7 March 1952) is an Antiguan retired cricketer who represented the West Indies cricket team between 1974 and 1991. Batting generally at number three[5][6][7] in a dominant West Indies side, Richards is widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen of all time.[8][9][10][11][12] Richards helped win his team the 1979 Cricket World Cup.
